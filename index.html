Kargil Vijay Diwas

26 July 2024

A tribute to India's brave soldiers

Kargil Vijay Diwas, observed every year on July 26, commemorates the bravery and sacrifice of Indian soldiers during the Kargil War of 1999This day marks the successful conclusion of the conflict between India and Pakistan, which started in May 1999, with Indian forces reclaiming strategic positions in the Kargil sector of Jammu and Kashmir that had been infiltrated by Pakistani soldiers and terrorists.Kargil Vijay Diwas is a solemn occasion to honour the ultimate sacrifice made by Indian soldiers during the 1999 conflict. The battles fought in the challenging terrain of Jammu and Kashmir saw 527 Indian soldiers lose their lives. Despite the difficult conditions, the Indian Army displayed extraordinary bravery and successfully reclaimed the occupied positions, forcing the Pakistani forces to withdraw.
